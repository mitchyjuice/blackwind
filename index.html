Welcome to Black Wind.

Black Wind is a novel created by Alex Mitchell on the world of Esdon.
Esdon has a rich history of Kings and Queens but direct connections to the Gods itself. In our story, we follow Kai. Kai has just reached his eighteenth birthday and is ready to join the Royal Guard, the main batallion of his county and the kingdom of Esdon.
Kai is one of the strange kids from the town of Heart Wood, staying relatively hidden. As our story unfolds, we learn much about Kais family, his background and his family history that runs into the fabric of the kingdom itself.
As our story continues, we learn of Kai's strange abilities and his connection to much of the landscape and an old ancestral beacon that drives Kai and his brother Leo to create the small group 'Black Wind'
